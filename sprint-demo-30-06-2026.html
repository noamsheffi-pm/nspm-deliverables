Act 1 · Govern on a cadence Governance

Stop Discovering Schema Problems During Incidents

Schema Explorer Monthly Reports
The customer"Our schema sprawls silently. By the time we notice field explosion or a cost spike, it's already in production and we're debugging it live. We have no routine way to review where our data is growing."
What we shipped: Schema Explorer now produces a recurring monthly report, inside the product, with configurable retention. Schema and data-growth posture becomes something a team reviews on a cadence, not something they discover too late.

Monthly Report, In-Product

A recurring monthly snapshot of your schema lives in Schema Explorer. No export pipeline, no manual pull, no spreadsheet.

Configurable Retention

Choose how long reports are kept, so you can look back and see how schema and data growth trended over months.

Catch Sprawl Before It Costs

Field explosion and growth hotspots surface on a schedule, so governance becomes proactive instead of incident-driven.

Foundation for Cost Reviews

The recurring report is the surface monthly cost and governance reviews are built on, tying schema visibility to spend.

Act 2 · See your spend precisely Quota & Monetization

Read Your Usage the Way You Actually Manage It

Data Usage & Org Quota — Sharper Visibility
The customer"Daily usage bars are noisy when I'm planning a monthly budget. And our org totals are skewed by internal audit teams, so I can't trust the number I'm reporting up to finance."
What we shipped: usage and quota now read at the grain you manage by. Pick the aggregation that matches your planning horizon, see exactly which teams are heading for a block, and get org totals that exclude internal audit noise.

Choose Your Aggregation

Auto, Daily, Weekly or Monthly on Data Usage and the Quota Manager, so the view matches the budget cycle you plan against.

See Who's About to Block

New block-days and estimated-blocked-units columns, plus a max-usage progress bar, make pressure visible before it bites.

Trustworthy Org Totals

Internal _audit teams are excluded from org-level counters, so the number you report up is the number that's real.

Cleaner Olly Usage View

Olly usage reads correctly for paid accounts and no longer shows zero when units are reserved by a quota rule.

Coming Next · Route it Coming Next

Cost Routing Reaches Your Traces

TCO for SpansComing Next
The customer"You've let me control log costs at ingestion for years. Spans are now my biggest, most expensive stream, and I have no equivalent lever for them."
Backend is live (span metering, quota-event emission). The spans policy / priority-block UI is still in build, so this is a roadmap preview, not a live demo.

Span-Aware Metering

The quota engine now meters spans by TCO policy and emits quota-tier-change events, the same primitives as logs.

Quota-Triggered Demotion

Spans demote priority as quota fills, just like log routing. The cost-control model extends to traces.

Editor Landing Next

The spans policy and priority-block UI is in build; full end-to-end ships in an upcoming sprint.
