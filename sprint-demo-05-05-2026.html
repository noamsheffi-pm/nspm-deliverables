Dataspace · Datasets

Any Data Structure That Suits Your Team

User-Defined Datasets — Manual Routing Early Access

Give every team, domain, or business unit its own data, schemas, retention, access, and cost visibility — without splitting into multiple Coralogix accounts. Customers can now carve their own datasets under default/ and route TCO traffic into them: auth logs, payments logs, infra logs — each in its own dataset.

Named, Independently-Schemed Datasets

Create datasets like payments, security-audit, infra — each tracked in real time, no upfront schema design.

Manual Routing in TCO

Pick the target dataset directly inside the TCO policy editor. Default behavior preserved, no migration required.

Per-Dataset Access & Governance

PBAC controls who can read, append, or overwrite. Independent policies for stronger compliance and clean schemas.

Scoped Querying & Cost Attribution

source default/payments in Explore with scoped autocomplete; per-dataset ingestion volume in Data Usage for real domain-level cost visibility.

TCO

TCO Optimizer — Migration & Governance

TCO Bulk Migration: Convert & Delete

A four-year customer ask, finally closed. Customers sitting on hundreds — sometimes thousands — of legacy log overrides now have a one-click migration path to modern TCO policies, plus bulk-delete to clear the rest.

Convert to Policy

Bulk-select legacy log overrides and convert them to TCO policies in one action — no manual rewriting.

Bulk Delete

Clear stale, redundant, or migrated overrides without deleting them one at a time.

Closes the Override Story

The migration path from legacy overrides to TCO V2 policies is now complete and self-serve.

Reduced Support Load

Migration tickets that previously required CS or engineering hand-holding can now be resolved by the customer.

Pipeline Analyzer

Pipeline Analyzer — Polish & Power

Five Enhancements that Make Investigation Faster

Pipeline Analyzer levels up across the board: full label visibility, jump straight from analyzer to the parsing rule, expanded side-by-side compare in sample and streaming modes, and clearer feedback during capture. Less context-switching, faster debugging.

$l & $m Labels in View

Dynamic metadata labels (app, subsystem, severity) now visible in raw and parsed logs — closes the visibility gap during pipeline debugging.

Open Parsing Rule Navigation

Hover any rule in the analyzer, click "Open Parsing Rule" — lands directly on the rule in the Parsing Rules screen.

Side-by-Side Compare, Reimagined

Wider modal, Affected vs Applied filter, Next/Previous navigation, "Affected" chip in the rule dropdown — in both sample and streaming modes.

Loading & Countdown Polish

Reliable loading animation, countdown to the 10-minute / 2000-log limit, and end-of-session feedback for empty captures.

Quota Rules

System / Datasets Quota Reservation

System Insights Quota — Guarantee Ingestion of Operational Visibility Data

A new design that brings system datasets into the quota allocation model. Reserved capacity for operational insights means platform health and audit data are always ingested, even when application data is approaching quota limits — and sets the pattern future quota work will inherit.

Reserved Quota for System Datasets

Operational signals (Olly, audit, AI evals, system traces) keep flowing even when application data is throttled.

Per-Dataset Quota Limits

Dataset-level allocation column in Datasets Management — configure and see reserved capacity per dataset.

One Unified Allocation Model

System-dataset reservation slots into the Quota Rules entityType design. Quota, governance, and billing aligned on a single primitive.

Predictable Observability

Size application quota aggressively without risking the operational signals your incident response depends on.

Data Usage

Data Usage

Data Usage as a Dataset Early Access

Your data consumption metrics become a queryable dataset inside Coralogix. Build custom dashboards, set alerts on usage spikes, and run DataPrime queries against your consumption data — just like any other dataset. Open for early-adopter customers now.

Usage as First-Class Telemetry

Consumption events stream into the dataplan.usage_events dataset, alongside your logs, metrics, and traces.

Custom Dashboards on Consumption

Build the FinOps and capacity views your org actually needs — by team, application, dataset, or any field.

Alerts on Usage Spikes

Treat usage like any other signal: alert on anomalies, daily growth, or budget thresholds.

DataPrime-Native Analytics

Join usage with logs, traces, and metrics in a single DataPrime query. Diagnose cost drivers in the same surface where you debug performance.

Data Usage Insights with Olly AI Coming Soon

Ask Olly about your data usage in plain English: "which apps spiked this week", "where can I cut TCO", "is my quota sized right". Olly answers natively against your consumption data and turns the answer into an actionable recommendation: a TCO rule to add, a quota rule to tighten, or a quota size to adjust.

Ask in Plain English

"Why did payments spike on Tuesday?" Olly answers against your consumption data, no DataPrime required.

TCO Recommendations on Demand

Olly surfaces the highest-ROI TCO rules to add, with the expected ingestion savings spelled out.

Quota Sizing & Rule Tuning

Right-size org/team quotas and tighten rules based on actual usage patterns, not guesswork.

FinOps in the Same Surface

Cost visibility, cause analysis, and corrective action live in one conversation. No Sheets, no dashboards-of-dashboards.

Customizable Usage Reports — Export & Analyze Your Data Consumption

Export and customize your data usage reports with flexible filters by data type, application, and time range. Analyze consumption patterns and share reports across your organization without leaving Coralogix or stitching dashboards together in Sheets.

Self-Service Export

One click from the 3-dot menu opens a configurable export: 90-day range, multi-tab selection, auto-named files.

Three Formats, Two Depths

CSV / TSV for spreadsheets, JSON for pipelines. Overview for summaries, Detailed for per-entity-per-day breakdowns.

What You See Is What You Export

"Apply current filters" carries active filters and group-bys straight into the file — exports match the on-screen view exactly.

First-Class PAYG Filter

Pay-As-You-Go customers can isolate PAYG consumption across cards, chart, table, and export — clean reports for FinOps and billing.
