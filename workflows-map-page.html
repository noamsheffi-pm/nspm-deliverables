Orphans & Underused

  • /note-to-ticket — dual-mode, untested
  • /action-sweep — alternative to /tag-execute, untested
  • cx-board-competitive, cx-pmm-review, calendar-insights, poc-prioritizer — passive skills, hard to invoke

Visible Gaps

1. /spec-to-team-epic → /initiative-review has no closing loop

Once epics land in Jira, tracking depends on workplan + roadmap-sync. A scheduled epic-health check could fill the gap.

2. /release-distribute doesn't loop back to roadmap

After release, /roadmap-sync should run automatically to mark Notion cards Done.

3. /intelligence-brief → /problem-discover not auto-triggered

Brief surfaces patterns weekly; discovery runs quarterly. Consider a monthly auto-trigger.

4. /pm-mentor runs are passive

No clear weekly review loop tying coaching notes back to growth dimensions in ai-pm-reference.md.
